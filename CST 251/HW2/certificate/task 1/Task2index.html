SZA is n American contemporary R&B singer that encompasses
elements of rap, rock and soul through her music. Her first
studio album, Ctrl, launched at No. 3 on the Billboard 200. Active
through the 2010-2020's, SZA released a studio album, 3
extended plays, and 16 singles. She is signed to the hip hop
record label Top Dawg Entertainment. Her debut studio album
Ctrl made in 2017 charted into the top 40 of the Billboard Hot 100.
   

Fun Facts About SZA

      - SZA is pronounced “sizz-ah”.
      - Her real name is Solana Rowe.
      - She grew up in Maplewood, New Jersey.
      -She was the most-nominated female artist
      at the 2018 Grammy Awards.
      - When she was a high school sophomore, she
      was ranked 5th in the nation as a gymnast.
